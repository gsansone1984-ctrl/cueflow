About CueFlow
Giuseppe Sansone
Creator of CueFlow

I built CueFlow because I needed a teleprompter that was simple, clean, and aligned with the way I actually work.

I tried many tools, but none of them matched the workflow I was looking for.
So, I decided to build one: fast, distraction-free, and reliable.

CueFlow is free, and it will be free forever.
If you find it useful, feel free to share it with your community.
