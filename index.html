Creator • Logistics & Supply Chain

I built CueFlow because I needed a simple, clean teleprompter that matched the way I work. I tried several tools, but none had the exact features and workflow I wanted for my videos.

So I built the teleprompter I wanted to use: fast, distraction-free, and designed to work smoothly on phone, tablet, and desktop.

Thanks for stopping by.
— Giuseppe Sansone

Free to use

CueFlow is free and will stay free. If you find it useful, share it with someone who records videos, teaches online, or creates content.

Optional donation (for charity)

If you’d like, you can leave an optional donation. Every donation will be forwarded to a selected charity and I will publish a simple transparency log (date, amount received, amount donated, and proof of transfer).

Note: donations are optional and do not unlock features. CueFlow works the same for everyone.

Colophon

CueFlow is built with plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It’s deployed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) so you can add it to your home screen. On iOS, use “Add to Home Screen”. On Android, your browser may prompt you to install it.

Privacy

The words you type or paste into CueFlow are not saved and are not sent anywhere. When you refresh the page, your script is cleared. CueFlow does not use ads, and it does not store personal profiles.
