I built CueFlow because I needed a simple, clean teleprompter that matched the way I work. I tried several tools, but none had the exact features and workflow I wanted.

So I built the teleprompter I wanted to use: fast, distraction-free, and designed to work smoothly on phone, tablet, and desktop.

Free to use

CueFlow is free and will stay free. If it helps you, share it with your community.

Optional donation (for charity)

If you’d like, you can leave an optional donation. Every donation will be forwarded to charity with a simple transparency log and proof of transfer.

Privacy

Your text is not saved and not sent anywhere. Refreshing the page clears everything.
